Our Purpose

Raising the Bar for
Real Estate Standards

The Coimbatore Association of Realtors (COAREA) is a non-profit organization representing the interests of real estate professionals in the region. We are a member of the National Association of Realtors - India (NAR-INDIA).

We exist to regulate the practice of real estate brokerage, provide educational opportunities, and enforce a strict Code of Ethics. We act as the bridge between the government, developers, and the public.

Our Mission

To professionalize the industry and protect the consumer through integrity and knowledge.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted authority for real estate guidance in South India.
