More Than Just an Association

Coimbatore, known as the "Manchester of South India," has undergone a radical transformation. From a textile hub to a sprawling metropolis of IT parks, educational institutions, and luxury manufacturing, the demand for land and infrastructure has skyrocketed.

In the late 90s, this rapid growth brought chaos. Unregulated brokers and unclear documentation were common risks. COAREA (Coimbatore Association of Realtors) was born out of the necessity to bring order to this chaos.

Today, we are a collective of the city's finest developers, consultants, and legal experts. We don't just facilitate transactions; we act as the guardians of property rights. Whether it's a commercial lease in Avinashi Road or a residential plot in Saravanampatti, a COAREA seal ensures the deal is legitimate.

Our Strategic Pillars

The core principles that drive our everyday operations.

Our Mission

To create a regulated, transparent, and ethical real estate ecosystem in Coimbatore. We aim to professionalize the industry by educating agents on RERA laws, protecting consumer rights against fraud, and fostering mutual trust between buyers, sellers, and government bodies.

Our Vision

To be the gold standard for real estate practice in South India, establishing a market where every transaction is safe, swift, and transparent for the common man.

Code of Ethics

We strictly enforce NAR-India guidelines. Any member found violating trust is subject to immediate disciplinary action.

Policy Advocacy

We represent the realtor interests to the DTCP and Corporation, ensuring city planning laws are practical and fair.

The Fraternity

Building a brotherhood of 500+ professionals who support, mentor, and collaborate with each other instead of competing cut-throat.

Functional Wings

Grievance Cell

A dedicated committee that mediates disputes between agents, or between clients and agents. We aim to resolve conflicts amicably out of court, saving time and money for all parties.

Education Wing

Focuses on upskilling members through regular workshops on Digital Marketing, RERA Compliance, GST Filing, and Soft Skills training.

Youth Wing

Empowering the next generation of realtors. This wing focuses on adopting new technologies, PropTech integration, and modern sales strategies.

Our Journey

1998

The Genesis

A small group of 10 visionary brokers gathered at the Cosmopolitan Club to discuss the lack of standards in the market. This informal meeting planted the seed for what would become COAREA.

2005

Formal Constitution

COAREA was formally registered as a society under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act. The first Constitution and bylaws were drafted, mandating a written code of conduct for members.

2012

NAR-India Integration

A milestone achievement connecting Coimbatore realtors to the national network. COAREA became an official member of the National Association of Realtors - India, opening doors to global referrals.

2017

The RERA Era

With the introduction of the Real Estate Regulation Act, COAREA played a pivotal role in educating over 1,000 local agents on compliance, registration, and legal frameworks.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launch of the digital verification system, QR-code based ID cards for members, and an online public grievance redressal portal to further transparency.
