Building a Unified Realtor Community

The Indian real estate sector experienced rapid expansion in the early 2010s, creating immense opportunities. However, the concerns of professionals often remained underrepresented.

To address this, industry leaders formed NAR India in 2008, followed by the establishment of COAREA in 2011 to represent real estate professionals in the Coimbatore region.

Starting with just 4 founding members, COAREA has grown into a 50+ member strong association, including TNRERA-registered brokers and property consultants, making it one of the most credible realtor networks in the region.

Our Journey

2011

The Genesis

A small group of 10 visionary brokers gathered at the Cosmopolitan Club to discuss the lack of standards in the market. This informal meeting planted the seed for what would become COAREA.

2014

Formal Constitution

COAREA was formally registered as a society under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act. The first Constitution and bylaws were drafted, mandating a written code of conduct for members.

2018

NAR-India Integration

A milestone achievement connecting Coimbatore realtors to the national network. COAREA became an official member of the National Association of Realtors - India, opening doors to global referrals.

2020

The RERA Era

With the introduction of the Real Estate Regulation Act, COAREA played a pivotal role in educating over 1,000 local agents on compliance, registration, and legal frameworks.

2026

Digital Transformation

Launch of the digital verification system, QR-code based ID cards for members, and an online public grievance redressal portal to further transparency.
